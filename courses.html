Our Courses

We offer a variety of courses to help you prepare for your exams and improve your English skills.

PTE Academic / UKVI / Core

Comprehensive coaching for all versions of the PTE exam. Includes mock tests, detailed feedback, and proven strategies.

IELTS Preparation

Full preparation course for IELTS Academic and General Training modules, covering all sections: Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking.

TOEFL iBT

In-depth TOEFL preparation focusing on all four sections to help you achieve a high score for university admissions.

Duolingo English Test

Targeted training for the Duolingo English Test, covering all question types and providing ample practice.

GRE Verbal Reasoning

Focused preparation for the GRE Verbal section, including Reading Comprehension, Text Completion, and Sentence Equivalence.

Spoken English

Improve your fluency, pronunciation, and confidence with our practical Spoken English courses, suitable for all levels.
